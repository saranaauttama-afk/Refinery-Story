# Refinery Story Mobile - Art Roadmap

## Goal

Prepare the current game for a future low poly industrial / miniature diorama
visual pass without changing UI structure, gameplay, or data systems.

## Estimated Scope

Planned unique asset count from [ASSET_LIST.md](/d:/My%20App/New-Refinery-Story/docs/ASSET_LIST.md:1):

- Total: `130`
- P0-heavy assets are concentrated in the Home (Refinery) loop
- P1 assets complete current system coverage
- P2 assets add content depth and presentation polish

## Recommended Production Order

1. Core playfield readability
2. Buildings used in the refinery grid
3. Product and resource iconography
4. Staff portraits and assignment-support visuals
5. Business-system glyphs and reusable contract assets
6. Achievement and event content assets
7. Backgrounds and celebration polish

  support screens

## Production Advice

- Do not start with event art before the building family is locked.
- Do not create detailed backgrounds before the grid plinth system is approved.
- Do not overproduce one-off illustrations if a reusable glyph solves the need.
- Keep all exports aligned to the Art Bible before scaling production.

## Risk Notes

- The current app still uses text-forward UI, so asset readability at small
  sizes matters more than painterly detail.
- Home (Refinery) is the highest-risk screen because it combines the grid,
  resource bar, product actions, modal sheets, and progression cards.
- Staff and Business are the next most valuable screens because they are
  currently the most text-heavy.
